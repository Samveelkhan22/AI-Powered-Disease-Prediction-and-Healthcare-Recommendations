Frequently Asked Questions

You can schedule an appointment through our website by filling out the appointment form, calling our office during business hours, or sending us an email. We typically respond to appointment requests within 24 hours on business days.

We accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, Medicare, and Medicaid. Please contact our office to verify whether we accept your specific insurance plan.

For your first appointment, please bring your insurance card, a form of photo ID, a list of current medications, any relevant medical records or test results, and payment for your copay or deductible. Arriving 15 minutes early to complete paperwork is recommended.

You can access your medical records through our secure patient portal. If you haven't set up your portal account yet, please contact our office for assistance. You can also request physical copies of your records by filling out a medical records release form.
